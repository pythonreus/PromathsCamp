Project Submissions

Show Us Who You Are!

This is your opportunity to create something amazing that represents you. Build a portfolio website that tells your story, showcases your skills, and demonstrates what you've learned.

The best portfolios are personal, authentic, and reflect the creator's unique journey.

Project Requirements: Portfolio Website

Create a personal portfolio website that showcases who you are and what you aspire to achieve. This is your chance to impress us with your skills!

Required Elements:
  • Your images must appear on the portfolio
  • Your name must be prominently displayed
  • "About Me" section telling us who you are and your future aspirations
  • Contact information must be included
Technical Requirements:
  • HTML & CSS must be used
  • JavaScript must be implemented (animations, interactivity, etc., anything you can do with js but it must be used)
  • Visually appealing design - make it beautiful!
Recommended Additional Content:
  • Work experience (if any)
  • Your skills (technical and soft skills)
  • Your hobbies and interests
  • Achievements and projects
Important Rules:
  • AI-generated code is NOT allowed - any code suspected to be from AI may result in no certificate being issued
  • You can use AI for learning purposes only, but all submitted code must be your own
  • The portfolio should tell your unique story and showcase your personality
Tips for Success:
  • Be creative and make it uniquely yours
  • Show us what you hope to achieve in the future
  • Use clean, organized code that you understand
Example Portfolio Structure:
Home/Hero Section (with your name and photo)
About Me (your story, aspirations)
Experience/Skills Section
Hobbies/Interests
Contact Section
Before Submitting, Check:

Deadline: Submit before the program ends. You can update your submission until the final deadline.

Certificate Eligibility: A successful portfolio submission is required to receive your completion certificate.
